About

I'm currently doing a Master's degree in Robotics at the Swiss Federal Institute of Technology, EPFL . Robotics is a very interesting field as it requires good knowledge of both the hardware side of a project (Electrical, Mechanical, Material engineering) and also the Software development side. To improve on the latter, I'm also doing a Minor in Data Science at EPFL. In my spare time I like to tinker with 3D printing and IoT projects. I also work as an engineering consultant part time. For fun, I like to explore new countries and I don't hesitate to pack my backpack for a quick adventure when I have the opportunity to do so.
